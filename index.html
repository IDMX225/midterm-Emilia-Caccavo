Honey Bee Bear Creations

Honey bee bear is a crochet plushie and the mascot for honey bee creations. They are about 7 inches tall and made with soft, velvet yarn. Honey Bee creations is about handmade, one of a kind plushies. We specialize in both croceht and sewn stuffed animals, art dolls, and plushies. They vary in design, size, and color. Each one has their own name, birthday, and personality. I first started making plushies 2 years ago. My first sewn plushie was a small white poodle dog. It had a ton of flaws, including holes and lopsided pieces. I have come a long way since and I absolutely love making plushies. I can't wait to come up with more designs and share my creations with more and more people.
